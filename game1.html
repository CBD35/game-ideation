The Call of the Void
Kingdom of Gyona — Year of Darkness

The Void came without warning. One morning the sky above the Kingdom of Gyona turned the colour of a bruise, and by nightfall the capital had fallen silent. Not conquered — erased. The Void swallowed the walls, the markets, the throne, and the people who sat upon it.

From the darkness poured creatures that had no name in any living tongue. Goblins and wolves from the outer woods, witches grown bold, trolls that shook the mountain passes, pirates who no longer feared the sea — all of them drawn toward the spreading shadow, corrupted by it, serving it without knowing why.

And at the centre of it all: the Void Lord. An ancient darkness that has waited centuries for a kingdom weak enough to consume.

Gyona sent out one last call before the Void closed over it entirely. Three heroes heard it. Whether they were brave, or foolish, or simply had nothing left to lose — they answered.

You are one of them. Dispel the Void. Save what remains.
